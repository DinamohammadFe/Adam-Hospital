What is IVF?

In vitro fertilization (IVF) is a procedure designed to improve fertility in patients who have not been successful with ovulation induction or intrauterine insemination, or who are not candidates for those more conservative treatments. Eggs are surgically removed from the ovaries and fertilized with sperm in a laboratory environment. The fertilized egg, i.e. embryo, is then transferred into the uterus.

Advantages of IVF

IVF has helped millions of families worldwide. With advanced technology and tailored care, this treatment provides the best chance to overcome infertility and achieve the dream of parenthood.

Creating Multiple Healthy Embryos
+

During IVF, multiple embryos are often created. Freezing allows you to preserve high-quality embryos for future attempts, reducing the need for repeated ovarian stimulation cycles.

Increased Pregnancy Success Rates
+

Having frozen embryos available gives you multiple opportunities for pregnancy from a single IVF cycle, significantly improving your overall chances of success.

Reduced Physical and Emotional Stress
+

Frozen embryo transfer cycles are less invasive and stressful than fresh IVF cycles, as they don't require ovarian stimulation or egg retrieval.

Cost-Effective Treatment
+

Using frozen embryos is more cost-effective than starting a new IVF cycle, as it eliminates the need for medication, monitoring, and egg retrieval procedures.

Flexibility in Family Planning
+

Frozen embryos allow you to plan your family on your timeline, whether you want to space pregnancies or delay childbearing for personal or medical reasons.

IVF Step by step

Initial Consultation & Testing

Meet with our fertility specialist to discuss your medical history, undergo comprehensive fertility testing, and create a personalized IVF treatment plan tailored to your specific needs.

Ovarian Stimulation

You'll take fertility medications to stimulate your ovaries to produce multiple eggs. Regular monitoring with blood tests and ultrasounds ensures optimal timing for egg retrieval.

Egg Retrieval

A minor outpatient procedure performed under sedation to collect mature eggs from your ovaries using ultrasound-guided needle aspiration. The procedure typically takes 20-30 minutes.

Fertilization

Retrieved eggs are fertilized with sperm in our advanced laboratory using either conventional IVF or ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection) depending on your specific situation.

Embryo Development

Fertilized embryos are cultured in our laboratory for 3-5 days while our embryologists monitor their development. The highest quality embryos are selected for transfer.

Embryo Transfer

One or more healthy embryos are carefully transferred to your uterus using a thin catheter. This simple, painless procedure is performed without anesthesia and takes just a few minutes.
