Understanding Male Infertility

Male infertility is more prevalent than most people realize, and it's a common misconception that infertility predominantly affects women. In reality, almost half of all fertility issues originate from male-related factors.

Our team of experienced andrologists and fertility specialists work together to provide personalized treatment plans.

A myriad of factors, such as hormonal imbalances, testicular damage or malformations, genetic factors, and environmental influences, can cause infertility in males. Age-related causes of infertility also play a significant role, as do certain diseases like diabetes, liver disease, and kidney disease
